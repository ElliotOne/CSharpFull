Naming Conventions For Types :

ex : I Love Programmnig , First Name


Camel case
	>>> iLoveProgramming , firstName

Pascal or upper camel case
	>>> ILoveProgramming , FirstName

Underscore or snake case
	>>> i_love_programming , first_Name

kebab case
	>>> i-love-programming , first-name




Note :
1. readability over breavity (GetProductCode instead of GetProCod)
2. Avoid long lines of code
3. Avoid number and special marks in naming
4. Don't use keywords or similar words
